- **Materials Processing:** Spark Plasma Sintering (SPS), powder preparation, and sample preparation for thermoelectric measurements  
- **X-ray Diffraction (XRD):** Hands-on training and operation of [XRD_Empyrean PANalytical](https://www.ul.ie/media/37754/download?inline) for phase identification and crystallographic analysis, including diffraction data interpretation using HighScore software  
- **Field Emission Scanning Electron Microscopy (FESEM) and Energy Dispersive X-ray Spectroscopy (EDX):** Hands-on training in microstructural imaging and compositional analysis using [Hitachi SU-70 FESEM with Oxford Instruments EDS](https://www.ul.ie/media/37741/download?inline), including secondary electron imaging, backscattered electron contrast, and elemental mapping 
- **Seebeck Coefficient and Electrical Conductivity Measurements:** Hands-on operation of [Linseis LSR-1](https://www.linseis.com/en/instruments/lsr-thermoelectrics/lsr-l32/) for simultaneous and automated measurement of Seebeck coefficient and electrical conductivity of thermoelectric materials under controlled temperature conditions
- **Differential Scanning Calorimetry (DSC) and Thermogravimetric Analysis (TGA):** Hands-on thermal analysis for investigating phase transitions, decomposition behavior, and thermal stability of materials under controlled temperature programs
- **Data Analysis and Scientific Documentation:** OriginPro, MATLAB, Python, LaTeX, and Overleaf
